Sunflower Welcome Sign Craft

Materials Needed:

  • Wooden pallet sign or a large wooden board
  • Acrylic paint (yellow, brown, green, and white)
  • Paintbrushes
  • Sealer (outdoor if the sign will be placed outside)
  • Sandpaper (optional, for distressing)
  • Stencil for the word "Welcome" (optional)
  • Pencil

Instructions:

  1. Start by lightly sanding your wooden pallet sign or board if necessary.
  2. Paint the background of the sign with white acrylic paint and let it dry completely.
  3. Sketch or stencil the word "Welcome" at the bottom or center of the sign.
  4. Paint the word "Welcome" with a contrasting color to make it stand out.
  5. Draw or use a template to create sunflower shapes on the sign. Position them around the word "Welcome" or as desired.
  6. Paint the sunflowers with yellow petals and brown centers. Add green leaves to fill in any empty spaces.
  7. Once all paint has dried, apply a coat of sealer to protect your sign, especially if it will be used outdoors.
  8. Allow the sealer to dry according to the product instructions.
  9. Your Sunflower Welcome Sign is now ready to display!
