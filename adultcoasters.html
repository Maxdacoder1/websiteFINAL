Mason Jar Lid Coasters

Materials Needed:

  • Mason jar lids (the ring and the flat part)
  • Adhesive cork shelf liner
  • Scissors
  • Pencil
  • Acrylic paint & paintbrush (optional)
  • Clear spray sealer (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Start by tracing the mason jar lid onto the cork shelf liner to get the correct size for the coaster insert.
  2. Cut out the cork circles you've traced. You may want to cut just inside the line you've drawn so the cork fits nicely inside the lid without sticking out.
  3. If you decide to paint your mason jar lids, do so before inserting the cork. Let the paint dry completely.
  4. Insert the cork circles into the mason jar lids. If your cork is adhesive, remove the backing and stick it to the inside of the lid. If it's not adhesive, you can use a bit of glue to secure it.
  5. Optional: Once the cork is in place and if you've painted the lids, spray a coat of clear sealer over the entire lid to protect the paint from moisture.
  6. Your mason jar lid coasters are now ready to use!
