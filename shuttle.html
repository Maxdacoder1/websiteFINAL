Paper Roll Space Shuttle Craft

Materials Needed:

  • Toilet paper roll
  • White paint
  • Blue paint
  • Yellow paint
  • Paintbrush
  • Black marker
  • Scissors
  • Glue
  • Construction paper (various colors)

Instructions:

  1. Start by painting the toilet paper roll white and let it dry.
  2. Once dry, add blue and yellow details to resemble the space shuttle's windows and features.
  3. Use a black marker to outline the windows and add any additional details.
  4. Cut out a cone shape from construction paper for the top of the shuttle and glue it to one end of the paper roll.
  5. Cut out two wing shapes and a tail fin from construction paper. Paint them if desired.
  6. Glue the wings on each side of the paper roll and the tail fin at the back.
  7. Allow all glued parts to dry completely.
  8. Your paper roll space shuttle is now ready for imaginative play!
