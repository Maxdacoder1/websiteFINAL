Paper Ball Garland DIY

Materials Needed

  • Colorful paper (construction paper or scrapbook paper)
  • String or yarn
  • Scissors
  • Glue
  • Hole punch

Instructions

  1. Cut the paper into equal-sized strips.
  2. Form a loop with a strip and glue the ends together.
  3. Continue adding paper strips, forming them into loops and gluing them together until you have a ball shape.
  4. Once the glue is dry, punch two holes on opposite sides of the ball.
  5. Thread the string or yarn through the holes to add the paper ball to your garland.
  6. Repeat the process to create more paper balls and add them to the garland.
