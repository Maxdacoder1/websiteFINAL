Paper Circle Fox Craft

Materials Needed:

  • Orange, white, and black paper
  • Scissors
  • Glue
  • Black marker
  • Circle punch (optional)
  • Googly eyes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cut out two circles from the orange paper for the fox's body and head.
  2. Cut out one smaller white circle for the face and two smaller black circles for the ears.
  3. Glue the white circle onto the larger orange circle (the head).
  4. Glue the black circles onto the top of the head for ears.
  5. Use a black marker to draw the fox's nose and mouth on the white circle.
  6. If you have googly eyes, glue them above the nose. Otherwise, draw the eyes with the black marker.
  7. Cut out four long strips from the orange paper for the legs and fold them accordion style.
  8. Glue the legs to the bottom of the second larger orange circle (the body).
  9. Finally, glue the head on top of the body.
  10. Your paper circle fox is now complete!
