DIY Paper Box Strawberry

Materials Needed:

  • Red paper (construction paper preferred)
  • Green paper (for leaves)
  • Black marker or pen (for seeds)
  • Scissors
  • Glue or double-sided tape
  • Ruler (optional)
  • Pencil (for tracing and marking)

Instructions:

  1. Cut a square piece of red paper to start your box.
  2. Fold the paper in half diagonally, unfold, and repeat in the other direction.
  3. Bring each corner of the square to the center of the paper.
  4. Fold each side's point to the edge of the central square and secure with glue or tape.
  5. Draw seed-like dots over the red paper with a black marker.
  6. Cut and attach a small piece of green paper on top to represent leaves.
  7. Gently open the folds to create a box shape.
  8. Make any final adjustments and add additional decorations as desired.
