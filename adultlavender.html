Lavender Bath Bombs

Materials Needed:

  • 1 cup baking soda
  • 1/2 cup citric acid
  • 1/2 cup Epsom salts
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 2 tbsp almond oil or melting coconut oil
  • 3/4 tsp water
  • 2 tsp lavender essential oil
  • A few drops of food coloring (optional)
  • Dried lavender flowers (optional)
  • Bath bomb molds or silicone molds

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together baking soda, citric acid, Epsom salts, and cornstarch.
  2. In a separate small bowl, mix the oil, water, lavender essential oil, and food coloring.
  3. Very slowly add the liquid mixture to the dry ingredients, stirring constantly. If the mixture starts to fizz or foam, slow down.
  4. If using, add dried lavender flowers to the mixture.
  5. Quickly press the mixture into bath bomb molds. Make sure to pack the mixture tightly into the molds.
  6. Allow the bath bombs to dry for at least 24 hours before removing them from the molds.
  7. Once dry, carefully remove the bath bombs from the molds.
  8. Store the bath bombs in a dry place until you're ready to use them.
  9. To use, simply drop one bath bomb into a bathtub filled with warm water and enjoy the relaxing lavender scent.
