Bouquet of Hearts Card

Materials Needed:

  • Colored paper (various colors)
  • Scissors
  • Glue
  • Green paper for the stems
  • Marker or pen
  • Cardstock or construction paper for the card base

Instructions:

  1. Cut out heart shapes from the colored paper. These will form the flowers of your bouquet.
  2. Cut thin strips of green paper to make the stems.
  3. Glue the stems onto your cardstock or construction paper, arranging them like a bouquet.
  4. Glue the heart shapes at the top of each stem. You can layer different colors or sizes of hearts for a more dynamic look.
  5. Once the glue is dry, use a marker or pen to add details like leaves or a bow around the stems.
  6. Write a sweet message inside the card to complete your Valentine's Day bouquet card.
